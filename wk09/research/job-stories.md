## Job Stories

## Job Story 1: Task 4
When I am seeing what I need to do for the week ahead,
I want to be able to see what assignments are due that week,
So I can concentrate on them
Because I have to submit them before the deadlines.

## Job Story 2: Task 3
When I have completed a task,
I want to be able to delete it from my to-do list,
So I can stay organised,
Because I don't want my list to be cluttered with jobs I've already done
